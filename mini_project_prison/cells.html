Cells Available

Very Lower Class Cell

Prisoners with 1-7 days punishment are kept in this cell.As per the terms of government prisoners are punished here.Prisoners who have done crimes such as minor fights,anti government acts etc. are punished in this cell.

Lower Class Cell

In this Cell we keep the prisoners with punishment of 2-7 weeks.Prisoners are provided foods and space by the cell. Prisoners with crime such as pick pockets,attend to murder,theft and etc. are kept here.

Average Class Cell

In this Cell we keep the prisoners with punishment of 2-10 years.Prisoners are provided foods and space by the cell as well visitors are allowed to visit the prisoners once in every 2 weeks. Prisoners with crime such as murder,rape,narcotic acts and etc. are kept here.

High Class Prison

In this Cell we keep the prisoners with punishment of 11years and upto life inprisonment.Prisoners are provided foods and space by the cell as well visitors are allowed to visit the prisoners once in every 6 weeks. Serial killers, Terrorists, Physco killers and etc. are kept here.
